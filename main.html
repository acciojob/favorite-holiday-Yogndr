Manali


Manali is a popular hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its stunning natural beauty and adventure activities. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush valleys, it attracts travelers for trekking, skiing, and a peaceful escape from the hustle of city life.

  1. Rohtang PassFamous for its breathtaking views and snow activities like skiing and snowboarding.
  2. Solang ValleyKnown for adventure sports such as paragliding and zorbing.
  3. Hadimba TempleA historical temple surrounded by cedar forests, dedicated to the goddess Hadimba.
  4. Old ManaliA quaint area with cafes and old-style architecture, perfect for relaxation.
  5. Beas River Ideal for river rafting and a peaceful retreat along the riverbanks.
  • Madra A traditional Himachali dish made with chickpeas, flavored with aromatic spices.
  • Siddu A steamed bread stuffed with a mixture of grated vegetables or meat.
  • Trout Fish A popular dish in Manali, served with a tangy marinade.
  • Apple Cider Manali is known for its apple orchards, and the apple cider is a must-try drink.
